About

Helheim Pinball is a score-based game in which the player has a ball that is propelled into a play area hitting ous lights, bumpers, ramps, and other targets in the play area. The player must use the “paddles” to juggle the ball and score points all while preventing the ball from falling into the drain. The player’s goal is to get the highest score possible. There are longboats that periodically appear and move across the area. If the player hits the longboats with the ball they can score more points. There are also runes placed throughout the play area for the player to gain double points.
