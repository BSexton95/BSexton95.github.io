About

"Party Palace" is a short horror game where you play as a boy attending Ryan's birthday party. Because you forgot to bring a gift, you are sent home. But come to find out the doors are locked and there is no way out. While searching for a key, you encounter a menacing meat monster that chases you relentlessly. Now you must navigate your way around in hopes of finding a way out all while avoiding the relentless pursuit of the meat monster.
