About

Hamburger Hell is a first-person horde shooter where you play as the mighty Le Chef and use your signature weapon, The Pepper Grinder, to defeat waves of rebellious hamburgers. The Pepper Grinder gives you two ways to defend yourself against the greasy beasts. The Pepper Shot which allows you to mow down burgers with the spread of a shotgun and speed of a gatling gun; and The Hot Dog Missile which decimates every enemy in your way.
